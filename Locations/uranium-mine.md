# Uranium Mine
<hr>

![Map of the Uranium Mine from a top-down view (lighter = higher elevation, darker = lower elevation)](/images/locations/uraniumminemap.png)

The Uranium Mine was added in version [alpha 1.4](/#version-2376-alpha-14) alongside the Uranium update. The Uranium Mine only contains nodes that spawn rocks and large rocks. The Uranium Mine is only used as a location to mine in. To access the Uranium Mine, you must have an `Iron Pickaxe` and an `IMD-5 Geiger Counter` and talk to the NPC named Dyatlov.
<br>

## Regular Uranium Rocks
The Regular Uranium Rock can be mined with any mining tool despite requiring an Iron Pickaxe to enter the mine in the first place. <br>

## Large Uranium Rocks
