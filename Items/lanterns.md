[!badge variant="success" text="Obtainable"]

## John O' Lantern
[!badge variant="danger" text="Unobtainable"]

The John O' Lantern was added in version [Alpha 1.4](/#version-2376-alpha-14) as part of the halloween update, and then was made unobtainable in version Alpha 1.4.1, a minor bug update version that also removed the halloween update.
