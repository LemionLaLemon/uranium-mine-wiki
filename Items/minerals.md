# Minerals
<hr>

Minerals can be obtained in the [Uranium Mine](/locations/uranium-mine/) by either mining [Regular Uranium Rocks](/locations/uranium-mine/#regular-uranium-rocks) or [Large Uranium Rocks](/locations/uranium-mine/#large-uranium-rocks). Minerals can be processed in the [Workshop](/locations/workshop/) to make other items. They were added in [Alpha 1.4](/#version-2376-alpha-14) as part of the uranium update.
