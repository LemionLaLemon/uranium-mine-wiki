# Explosives

!!!Tip
`Low Power Explosive`s are used for doing fun things, like blowing up terrain and players, and cannot be used to mine
<br>`High Power Explosive`s are use to blow up Large Uranium Rocks
!!!
<hr>

**Uranium Mine Simulator** has two types of explosives


## Low Power Explosive
[!badge variant="success" text="Obtainable"]

The `Low Power Explosive` looks like every average video game "Bomb". It allows you to chip off bits of terrain and kill players.<br>

## High Power Explosive
[!badge variant="success" text="Obtainable"]

The `High Power Explosive` looks like every average video game "TNT". It allows you to blow up Large Uranium Rocks found in the [Uranium Mine](/Locations/uranium-mine)<br>
